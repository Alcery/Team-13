Transportation

  • RideShare
    • Uber WAV supports riders who use wheelchairs or scooters that would like to travel to a specific location for a reasonable price. Drivers are certified to assist and keep the riders safe.
    • Lyft is a service similar to Uber, but you must have the mobile app to request a ride. You can change your app settings to enable the Access Mode, which will request vehicles that accommodate wheelchair riders.
  • Public Transportation
    • Valley Metro provides buses and a light rail that can be used by the public. Their vehicles are made to allow people who use wheelchairs to board with the assistance of employees from Valley Metro.
    • If a disability prevents people from utilizing the public transportation provided by Valley Metro, people may use Valley Metroâ€™s Dial-a-Ride service to request a bus to their specific location. A Personal Care Attendant must accompany the rider.
  • Personal Transportation
    • National Mobility Equipment Dealers Association helps users with disabilities find car dealerships that have vehicles which support the use of wheelchairs.
    • Valley Medical Supplies allows people to rent wheelchairs and transport chairs for a low price in Phoenix Arizona. The organization will assist in determining the right size of the wheelchair or transport chair.
