Transportation

    Uber

  • Uber WAV supports riders who use wheelchairs or scooters that would like to travel to a specific location for a reasonable price. Drivers are certified to assist and keep the riders safe.

    • Lyft

  • Lyft is a service similar to Uber, but you must have the mobile app to request a ride. You can change your app settings to enable the Access Mode, which will request vehicles that accommodate wheelchair riders.

    • Valley Metro

  • Valley Metro provides buses and a light rail that can be used by the public. Their vehicles are made to allow people who use wheelchairs to board with the assistance of employees from Valley Metro.

    • Valley Metro’s Dial-a-Ride

  • If a disability prevents people from utilizing the public transportation provided by Valley Metro, people may use Valley Metro’s Dial-a-Ride< service to request a bus to their specific location. A Personal Care Attendant must accompany the rider.

    • National Mobility Equipment Dealers Association

  • National Mobility Equipment Dealers Association helps users with disabilities find car dealerships that have vehicles which support the use of wheelchairs.

    • Valley Medical Supplies

  • Valley Medical Supplies allows people to rent wheelchairs and transport chairs for a low price in Phoenix Arizona. The organization will assist in determining the right size of the wheelchair or transport chair.

Provisions

Services that offer affordable food options, clothing for people with unique fitting needs, and basic services tailored to meet special needs.

    Local Food Bank

  • Provides a search engine that will help users find a local food bank.

    • Choose My Plate

  • Helps users learn of healthy eating habits and provides other information that can promote a healthy life.

    • Janines Alterations

  • A business that makes alterations on clothing and helps fit the needs of the requestor. This business is located at
    4000 N 7th St #124, Phoenix, AZ 85014
    .

    • The Fit Alterations

  • A business that makes alterations on clothing which can help fit the needs of the requestor. Have three different locations in Phoenix Arizona.

    The Travling Barbers

  • Helps individuals with disabilities find barbers that can accommodate their needs. Users may post requests for barbers and can post their own price.

    • 101 Phones

  • Categories phones to help meet different needs of people with disabilities. They also show other special needs devicesthat may help improve the lifestyle of people with disabilities. Products can only be ordered online.

Education

Organizations that guide people to create assistive technology or promote a healthy learning environment for people with disabilities.

    Raising Arizona Kids

  • Helps parents find schools in Arizona that can cater to the special needs of their children while offering a good learning environment where their children can grow.

    • Arizona Autism Services

  • Allows parents to book a therapist that can support various needs that a child may have. Parents can meet with an employee in person or through the web.

    AT Makers

  • Guides the community to create their own assistive technology and modifies toys that children with disabilities can enjoy. Also, open up events where the community can come together and make assistive technology.

    • ATIA

  • People who are interested in creating assistive technology can learn from professionals through their webinars. Offer several of these webinars at no cost.

    Joni and Friends

  • Incorporates fun for children with disabilities while also giving them hope and also allows them to get closer to Christ. Have multiple offices around the United States.

    • Mosaic

  • Inspired by faith to help people with disabilities learn of more possibilities for their future and also offers more independence to those who register to the Host Homes program.
