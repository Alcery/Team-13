Statistics for Disabled Americans

The American Community Survey (ACS) estimates the overall rate of people with disabilities in the US population in 2016 was 12.8%.

People with Disabilities, by Type in 2016

  • 6.6% Ambulatory
  • 4.8% Cognitive
  • 4.5% Independent Living
  • 3.5% Hearing
  • 2.5% Self-Care
  • 2.4% Vision

In 2016, 20.9% of people with disabilities lived in poverty, while 13.1% of people with disabilities lived in poverty.
